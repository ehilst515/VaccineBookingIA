Tips

Appointments will be filled very quickly, so try to get through the booking process as fast as possible. Specifically with Hy-Vee, the form can be filled all the way up to submission, where you can then change your appointment location. Fill out the form even if you think it's too late, then return to the location setter and keep an eye out for newly available Hy-Vee appointments.
